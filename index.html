Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. Any number divisible by 3 becomes "ping"
  2. Any number divisible by 5 becomes "pong"
  3. Any number divisible by 15 (or both 3 and 5) becomes "pingpong"
